Job creation and workforce development

Vision: All residents have the job training, basic skills, and access to services that they need to succeed in living wage employment and achieve financial stability

2020 targets

Underemployment
Reduce the underemployment rate from 20% to 15% by moving 3,000 individuals into better jobs
Living wage jobs
Increase the percentage of residents in jobs making a living wage from 39% to 45%, placing 700 people per year
Pipeline
Establish a unified workforce pipeline that supports, tracks, and places 5,000 residents in jobs
